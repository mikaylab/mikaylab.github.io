About

I am a thoughtful, playful, and integrative thinker that works as a design researcher and creative technologist. I use system-level thinking in my work to understand the root causes of user issues and identify levers for change. I employ service design, co-design, and participatory design methods to investigate these system conditions based on user experiences and knowledge of the system.

I hold a Master of Design in Integrative Design from the University of Michigan, and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from University of Wisconsin - Madison. I leverage the knowledge and skills I gained from these disciplines to increase digital inclusion and equity through user-centered design and community-engaged research.

Email me at mikaylabuford17@gmail.com, or connect with me on LinkedIn.
