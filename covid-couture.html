COVID Couture

Exploring the Intersection of Fashion and PPE

This summer, I have the privilege of working with Asta Roseway, Vaishnavi Ranganathan, and Teddy Seyed from Microsoft Resarch - Redmond to study the emergence of personal protective equipment (PPE) development by designers and makers in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The goal of our work is to document the work of these designers affects our perception and use of masks, as well as the design of future wearables.
