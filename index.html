Hi, I'm Mikayla Buford. I'm a graduate student in the Masters of Design program at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. I attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison for my Bachelors in Computer Science. I'm interested in the marriage of technology, art, and social justice through the lens of Afrofuturism.

Aside from being a programmer I enjoy exploring how creative technology can be applied to humanities and creating social awareness.
